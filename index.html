Breath of the Wild (Wii U)

Breath of the Wild is, without a doubt, my new favorite game of all time. Far above the games I've played that involve time travel (Chrono Trigger), games that evoked emotional depths that I didn't expect from a video game (Final Fantasy 3 and 7, I wept like a baby), and any other Zelda games before it (Ocarina of Time and Majora's Mask....frickin amazing). Yes, it's THAT GOOD.

Daniele Franklin
